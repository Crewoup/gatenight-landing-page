# GATENIGHT PRIVACY ADDENDUM
**Last Updated: 2026-03-31**

This Privacy Addendum ("Addendum") applies specifically to **GateNight**, a social platform for aviation professionals operated by Alnair Studio. This document supplements the [Alnair Studio Master Privacy Policy](https://www.alnair-studio.com/privacy).

## 1. IDENTITY VERIFICATION & KYC
To maintain a community of verified aviation professionals, we perform a Know Your Customer (KYC) process:
* **Real-time Verification:** Users are required to provide:
    1. A live photo taken with the **front camera** (for liveness and uniform detection).
    2. An image of their professional ID card taken with the **rear camera** (for identity and credential verification).
* **Data Minimization:** These verification images are processed in real-time to extract necessary information. We do **NOT** permanently store the raw images of your ID cards on our primary production servers. 
* **Manual Review:** If AI analysis is inconclusive, an encrypted Base64 representation of the image may be transmitted via secure internal channels for manual audit. These temporary files are deleted once the verification is complete.

## 2. AI IMAGE ANALYSIS
* **AI Engine:** GateNight utilizes Google Gemini to analyze:
    * **Front Camera Photos:** To determine liveness, presence of aviation uniforms, and selfie authenticity.
    * **Rear Camera Photos:** To verify the validity and professional details of the aviation ID card.
* **No Biometrics:** Our AI analysis is focused on attribute and text recognition (OCR). We do not extract, collect, or store any biometric templates or "faceprints" from your photos.

## 3. ITINERARY & LOCATION DATA
* **User-Controlled Itinerary:** Flight schedules and itineraries are uploaded voluntarily. You have full control over whether this information is public or private.
* **Granular Location Permissions:** We request GPS access via standard Android/iOS methods. You may choose to grant "Continuous" or "One-time" access. 
* **Location Privacy:** We **never** share your precise GPS coordinates with other users. We only use proximity data to provide general social notifications (e.g., "There are 3 users in Paris with you").

## 4. CONTENT MODERATION & SAFETY
* **Reporting Mechanism:** In the event of a user report or suspected violation of our terms, we retain conversation logs and relevant metadata as evidence for investigation.
* **Account Status:** Reported users may be set to "Inactive" pending manual review. We retain account status data to prevent bypass of our safety protocols.

## 5. DATA DELETION
* **Account Deletion:** Upon account deletion, your profile and personal identifiers are removed from our active systems. 
* **No Recovery:** Please note we do not currently offer a backup recovery period; once deleted, data cannot be restored.

## 6. AGE RESTRICTION
GateNight is intended for users of legal working age in the aviation industry. You must be at least **18 years of age** to use this service.
